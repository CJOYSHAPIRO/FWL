WHO WE ARE

"Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, commited citizens can change the world, indeed, it is the only thing that ever has."
-Margaret Mead

The Future World Leaders, (FWL) is a non- Profit organization devoted to empowering youth that are currently receiving services from the department of Youth Protection (DYP). This includes youth who are living in foster homes, group homes, residential units & also receiving services at home in the community. Currently we have 6 participating youth in the Montreal area. We empower the youth by fueling them with leadership tools and a life changing experience, while allowing them to immerse themselves in doing something greater for the less fortunate children in communities abroad. 

In 2016-2017 we launched our first Future World Leader’s pilot project, a humanitarian trip to India, where our youth helped build a school. The program proved to be successful. Both participants expressed that the program had a positive impact in their lives. One of the two youth has chosen to continue participating with us. Since his enrollment with Future World Leaders, he has taken school more seriously, pursued personal goals like getting his driver licence, has more positive connections in the community and has expressed that this program helps him stay out of trouble while doing something he likes to do, helping others. He also expressed that this program is in line with his future goals. He hopes to become a leader in his community and shape his community into a better place to live.
